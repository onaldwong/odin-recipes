Peppered Shrimp Alfredo

Submitted by Jenn Zeller · Updated on February 4, 2025

This yummy peppered shrimp in an Alfredo sauce recipe includes portobello mushrooms and red bell pepper for a rich, flavorful pasta dish.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces penne pasta
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 8 ounces portobello mushrooms, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 (15-ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
  • ½ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup chopped flat-leaf parsley

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne pasta and cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente (8 to 10 minutes). Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter with olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in red bell pepper, mushrooms, and garlic. Cook over medium-high heat until soft, about 2 more minutes.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until firm and pink.
  5. Stir in Alfredo sauce, Romano cheese, and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly, until thickened (about 5 minutes).
  6. Season with cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Stir in cooked penne pasta until well coated.
  8. Serve hot, sprinkled with chopped flat-leaf parsley.
